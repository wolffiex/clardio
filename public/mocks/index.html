Design Notes

  • • All mockups show sample data: 165W/82rpm current, 180W/85rpm target
  • • Orange indicates under target, green indicates at or over target
  • • HR and elapsed time in top left (secondary info)
  • • Countdown timer visible in all layouts
  • • Bottom right reserved for YouTube PiP overlay
  • • Coach message area in center
  • • Each mockup includes examples of both under and over target states
